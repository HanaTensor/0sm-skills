================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This document provides a detailed conceptual and structural exposition of the
0-Sphere model, intended as a supplementary companion to the main manuscript.
While the primary paper focuses on formal derivations and concise theoretical
claims, this supplement elaborates on the underlying motivation, ontological
assumptions, and mechanistic interpretation of the model.

Particular emphasis is placed on the role of line-integral-based interactions,
internal phase dynamics, photon-mediated synchronization, and the emergence of
gravitational-like behavior without introducing additional mediators. Open
questions, limitations, and directions for future investigation are explicitly
identified to clarify the scope and status of the framework.
